Conjugation






Turkish conjugation is very easy, the conjugation uses markers instead of stem changes or additional words. These markers(called inflectional markers) always come last if several markers are appended to one verb.

Negation and Questions

To form the negative, add another marker in position 1(check the markers page if you do not understand this), which is M². However, for forming negation in the tense "Aorist," M²z is used instead. The "z" is excluded in First-Person singular and plural in present tense.

Yemek - To eat
Yememek - To not eat

Yerim - I eat
Yemem - I dont eat

Yersin - You eat
Yemezsin - You dont eat

Forming a question is slightly different from other markers, the question marker is actually applied as a different word after the verb. However, the marker still follows vowel harmony based on the original verb(and personal markers are added to the question marker instead if you're asking "Am I," "Are you," etc).

M⁴ - Question marker

Yedin - You ate
Yedin mi? - Did you eat?
